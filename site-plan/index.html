Site Plan Overview

Project Overview

This site plan outlines the structure and content strategy for my sister-in-law's nail tech business website. The goal is to create an engaging and user-friendly site that showcases services, pricing, portfolio, and contact information to attract and retain clients.

The Site Name will be - nbk.co used to be quick to type. (Nails by Kim) is what the abbreviation will stand for.

The Site Purpose is to present a booking system for clients to schedule appointments for my sister-in-law and to show her past work, reviews and nail services she offers.

The Scenario Questions that will be answered are - 1. What Services can I get? 2. How can I book an appointment and review past works with pictures and user-created reviews? These will be answered by separating the site into 3 pages for specific reading on services/booking and an About Me page to show past work.

Color and Font Schemes

Color Schemes: The website will utilize a soft pastel color palette. The main color for the nav bar and footer will be -F08080

The secondary color for buttons and highlights will be -C85A5A

Accent colors for icons and links will be -D67570

Content Boxes will be using D67570 sharing the secondary color.

The background color will be an ivory color - FFFFFFF0 to not just use a flat white background.

Social Media Logos will also match similar colors to maintain brand consistency deciding between F4978E and FBC4AB.

Font Scheme

The primary font for headings, the navbar and the footer text will be - 'Gluten 500/400'.

The secondary font for body text will be 'Outfit 400-600'.

Special fonts like the hero font to use on the main page will be - Kapakana 400. Just for visual appeal over the hero image.

Fonts will be both set in black and white to make sure contrast isn't an issue with the color scheme.
